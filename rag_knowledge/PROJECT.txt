# FlipLearn — Flipped Classroom Platform: Complete User Guide

## What Is This Platform?
FlipLearn is a **Flipped Classroom web application** built for M.Tech CSE students and teachers.
In a flipped classroom model, students study instructional content (videos, notes) at home and use
class time for discussion and problem-solving. This platform supports:

- Video lecture hosting and watching
- Study material uploads (PDFs, docs)
- Quizzes and auto-grading
- Assignments with file submission
- Performance analytics powered by ML models
- An AI-powered chatbot (FlipLearn AI) for academic help

---

## How to Access the Platform

### Register (New Students)
1. Go to the home page and click **Register**.
2. Fill in your name, username, email, and password.
3. Click **Sign Up** — you are logged in automatically and taken to your Dashboard.

### Login (Existing Users)
1. Go to the home page and click **Login**.
2. Enter your username and password, then click **Login**.
3. You are taken to your **Dashboard**.
4. Note: The AI chatbot history is cleared on each login so every session starts fresh.

### Logout
- Click **Logout** from any page or the dashboard navigation bar.

---

## Dashboard
After login, your dashboard shows:
- Your enrolled subjects
- Recent video lectures
- Pending assignments
- Upcoming or recently taken quizzes
- Notifications
- A shortcut to the AI Chatbot

---

## Subjects

### How to View Subjects
- Navigate to **Subjects** from the top menu.
- All available subjects are listed (DS, PY, WD, CN, DSC, AIML).

### How to Enroll in a Subject (Students)
1. Go to **Subjects**.
2. Click **Enroll** next to the subject you want to join.
3. Once enrolled, you can see that subject's videos, materials, and quizzes.

---

## Videos

### How to Watch a Video Lecture
1. Go to **Videos** from the navigation menu.
2. You can filter by subject.
3. Click a video title to open the **Video Detail** page.
4. The video player will load. Watch the lecture.
5. Your watch history is automatically tracked.

### How to Upload a Video (Teachers Only)
1. Go to **Upload Video** from the teacher menu.
2. Fill in the title, select the subject, and choose the video file.
3. Click **Upload** — the video is saved and students can now see it.

---

## Study Materials

### How to View Study Materials
1. Go to **Materials** from the navigation menu.
2. Filter by subject if needed.
3. Click on a material to download or view it.

### How to Upload a Study Material (Teachers Only)
1. Go to **Upload Material**.
2. Fill in the title, select the subject, and attach the file (PDF, DOCX, etc.).
3. Click **Upload**.

---

## Quizzes

### How to Take a Quiz (Students)
1. Go to **Quizzes** from the navigation menu.
2. Click **Start Quiz** next to any available quiz.
3. Answer all multiple-choice questions.
4. Click **Submit** when done.
5. You are shown your **Quiz Result** with score, correct answers, and feedback.

### How to Create a Quiz (Teachers Only)
1. Go to **Create Quiz**.
2. Enter the quiz title, select the subject, and set the time limit.
3. Click **Save** to create the quiz.
4. You are taken to **Add Question** — add as many MCQ questions as needed.
5. Each question has: question text, option A/B/C/D, and the correct option.

### How to Add Questions to a Quiz (Teachers Only)
1. From the quiz list, click **Add Questions** for any quiz.
2. Fill in: question, option A, B, C, D, and correct answer.
3. Click **Add Question**. Repeat for more questions.

---

## Assignments

### How to View Assignments
1. Go to **Assignments** from the navigation menu.
2. You will see all current assignments with due dates.

### How to Submit an Assignment (Students)
1. Click **Submit** next to an assignment.
2. Upload your file and optionally add notes.
3. Click **Submit Assignment**.
4. Submission is recorded and the teacher is notified.

### How to Create an Assignment (Teachers Only)
1. Go to **Create Assignment**.
2. Fill in the title, description, subject, and due date.
3. Click **Create**.

### How to Grade an Assignment (Teachers Only)
1. Go to **Assignment Submissions** to see all submitted work.
2. Click **Grade** next to a submission.
3. Enter marks and feedback, then click **Submit Grade**.

---

## Analytics and Performance

### Student: My Performance
1. Go to **My Performance** from the navigation menu.
2. See your quiz scores, assignment grades, and subject-wise progress charts.

### Student Analytics (Teachers Only)
1. Go to **Analytics** from the teacher menu.
2. View class-wide statistics: average scores, quiz pass rates, assignment completion.
3. The ML model predicts student performance and highlights at-risk students.

### Analytics Page
- Shows bar charts and graphs for quiz attempts, assignment scores, and video watch history.
- Powered by a **Random Forest** ML model trained on classroom data.

## AI Chatbot (FlipLearn AI)

### How to Use the Chatbot
1. Click **AI Chat** or **FlipLearn AI** from the navigation menu or dashboard.
2. Select a subject from the dropdown (optional — helps get more focused answers).
3. Type your question in the input box and press **Send** or hit Enter.
4. The AI responds with a formatted answer using context from subject notes.
5. Suggested follow-up questions appear below each answer — click one to continue.

### What Can the Chatbot Help With?
- Explaining concepts from DS, PY, WD, CN, DSC, AIML subjects
- How to use this platform (just ask!)
- Step-by-step guides for any feature
- Code examples and algorithm explanations
- Quiz and assignment related academic questions

### Chatbot Tips
- The chat history resets every time you log in (fresh start).
- You can ask "How do I upload a video?" or "How do I submit an assignment?" and the AI will guide you.
- Typing in the subject box narrows down the answers.

## Common Questions

### Q: I forgot my password. How do I reset it?
Contact your system administrator or teacher to reset your password from the Django admin panel.

### Q: How do I know which subject a video belongs to?
Each video shows its subject tag. You can also filter the video list by subject.

### Q: Can I retake a quiz?
Yes. Go to **Quizzes**, find the quiz, and click **Start Quiz** again.

### Q: Where do I see my grade for an assignment?
Go to **My Performance** or check **Assignments** — graded submissions show the score and teacher feedback.

### Q: The chatbot is not answering about the platform. What do I do?
Ask directly, for example: "How do I upload a video?" or "How do I view my assignment grades?" The chatbot is trained on this guide and will assist you.

### Q: What subjects are available?
- **DS** — Data Structures
- **PY** — Python Programming
- **WD** — Web Development
- **CN** — Computer Networks
- **DSC** — Data Science
- **AIML** — Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning

### Q: How is my performance predicted?
Your quiz scores, assignment grades, and attendance data are fed into a trained **Random Forest** machine learning model that predicts your academic performance and flags areas for improvement.
