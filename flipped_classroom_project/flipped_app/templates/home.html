How It Works

The three-phase flipped learning cycle — before, during, and after class.

1
Before Class — Pre-learning

Students watch video lectures & download study materials before class. The platform records watch-time, completion % and access patterns per subject.

2
During Class — Active Practice

Students take auto-graded quizzes, submit assignments, and chat with the RAG AI Tutor. Teachers see live dashboards and give inline feedback.

3
After Class — ML Analysis

Random Forest models analyse engagement data to predict final exam scores, label performance (Good / Average / Poor) & flag at-risk students for early intervention.
