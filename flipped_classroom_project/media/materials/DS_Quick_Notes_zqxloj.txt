────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  Arrays & Strings
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  An array stores elements of the same type contiguously in memory.
  Access O(1), search O(n), insert/delete O(n).
  Multi-dimensional arrays, row-major vs column-major order.

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  Linked Lists
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  Singly, doubly and circular linked lists.
  Node structure: data + pointer(s). Insert/delete O(1) with a held
  pointer.
  Applications: LRU cache, undo systems, polynomial arithmetic.

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  Stacks & Queues
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  Stack – LIFO: push, pop, peek. Queue – FIFO: enqueue, dequeue.
  Circular queue, deque (double-ended queue).
  Applications: expression evaluation, BFS, call stack simulation.

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  Trees
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  Binary Tree, BST, AVL Tree, Red-Black Tree, B-Tree.
  Traversals: inorder, preorder, postorder, level-order.
  Height, diameter, lowest common ancestor (LCA).

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  Graphs
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  Directed & undirected graphs, adjacency matrix vs adjacency list.
  DFS, BFS, topological sort, Dijkstra, Bellman-Ford, Floyd-Warshall.
  Minimum spanning tree: Prim's and Kruskal's algorithms.

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  Sorting & Searching
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  Bubble, selection, insertion, merge, quick, heap sort.
  Time/space complexities and stability comparison table.
  Binary search, interpolation search, hashing and hash tables.
